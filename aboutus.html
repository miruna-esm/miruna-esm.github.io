The Shuffle Background

The Shuffle is a ska band formed in 2013 by vocalist Winona Rhiannon, guitarist Alex Merrick, drummer John Page, and saxophonist William May.  Alex and John had already been friends for a very long time and were looking for a lead singer to start a band together. They've met Winona in bar and started talking about music. Back then, Winona was passionate about tarot readings and was carrying her tarot cards in her bag. Starting as a joke, Alex and John asked her to do a reading. It was then when they have decided to call the band "The Shuffle", taken from the luck the cards could foresee for their band. Later on, in 2017, they've met William and have decided that the saxophone could help them achieve a more unique sound.
